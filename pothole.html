Automated Pothole Detection

Heavy Vehicle Tracking with YOLOv4 and DeepSORT

We use SOTA algorithms of object detection and tracking to complete our software. The YOLOv4 model was re-trained with the COCO dataset and an online Indian traffic dataset to generalise detection to the Indian traffic realm. The model is combined with tracking technologies of DeepSORT to ensure no one vehcile is detected 129081290382 of times.

Moving on to the software side of the project. We have adjusted our model thresholds to detect more false positives rather than avoiding them because no fugitive should get away. Once a truck is detected, 3 photos of the truck in motion are clicked which is then used to create a web-app report using Streamlit. We also run the image sthrough our ANPR API to ensure no avenues are left unchecked

Our project is very simple. To combat the movement of the most dangerous vehicles on the road we have created an automatic detection challan system. If you are on wrong route on wrong time of the day our software guarantees that you will not go unpunished. With SOTA algorithms, we use AI to make our roads truly safe
